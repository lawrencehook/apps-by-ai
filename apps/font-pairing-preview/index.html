The Art of Typography

Typography is far more than selecting a pretty font. It's about creating harmony, hierarchy, and readability that guides your readers effortlessly through content.

The right font pairing can elevate a design from amateur to professional. Contrast is key—pair a bold, expressive heading font with a clean, readable body font for the best results.

Consider the mood you want to convey. Serif fonts feel traditional and trustworthy, while sans-serif fonts appear modern and clean. Mixing them thoughtfully creates visual interest.
