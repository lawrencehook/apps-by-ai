About Deuteranopia

Deuteranopia is a type of red-green color blindness where the green-sensitive cone cells don't function properly. People with this condition have difficulty distinguishing between red and green colors. It's the most common form of color blindness, affecting approximately 6% of males and 0.4% of females.
